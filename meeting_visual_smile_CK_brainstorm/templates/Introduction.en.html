Technical settings

In order to ensure that participants can see and hear you correctly, please make sure that you comply with all the following points:

  • You are using a laptop or a desktop computer (not a smartphone)
  • You are using the latest version of the Chrome browser (Other browsers DO NOT WORK). If you are not using the latest version of Chrome please update your software.
  • if you use a VPN, turn it OFF.
  • Please close tabs (or applications) that consume bandwidth (e.g., Netflix, Youtube or download applications)
  • You have a good internet connection (prefer an ethernet connection to wifi or 4G)
  • You have headphones, preferably in-ear, to listen to other participants
  • You are in a correctly light room (beware of backlighting: avoid having a window or a light source behind you)
  • Please stay on the experiment window throughout the experiment and do not do anything else during the experiment
  • During the interactions you will not be able to see your own face. Therefore, make sure to adjust your camera at the beginning of the experiment, and tell the other participants if their face is not well centered in the middle of the screen
  • Please make sure that your face is in the center of the screen and that you are about 70cm away from the screen.
  • Elevate your computer/webcam with a stand or book to ensure your camera is centered at eye level (see picture bellow)
  • During the experiment, avoid changing your position drastically in order to remain centered and looking straight
  • During the experiment, avoid covering your face with your hands, or having hair on your face.
  • If you have problems during the experiment you can chat with the experiment send a message to the experimenter
  • Please take a close look at the following examples, make sure you are following all instructions correctly.
