What is Brainstorming?

In this study, we are investigating how people think and interact during creative tasks. To do this, you will take part in short idea-generation sessions with other participants, called brainstorming sessions.

Brainstorming means to come up with as many ideas as you can in a short amount of time. The goal of the experiment is therefore to be creative, spontaneous, and open-minded. There are no wrong answers â€” even unusual or surprising ideas are welcome.

With this in mind, please participate actively, and try to contribute with as many different and original ideas as you can during each session.
