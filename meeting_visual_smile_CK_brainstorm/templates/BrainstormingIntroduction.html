What is Brainstorming?

In this study, you will take part in short idea-generation sessions with other participants. These are called brainstorming sessions.

Brainstorming means coming up with as many ideas as you can in a short time. The goal is to be creative, spontaneous, and open-minded. There are no wrong answers â€” even unusual or surprising ideas are welcome.

We are investigating how people think and interact during creative tasks. So please participate actively, and try to contribute as many different and original ideas as you can during each session.
