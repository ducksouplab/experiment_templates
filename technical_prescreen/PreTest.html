At the start of the test, you will be told to remain silent for 12 seconds. After this, a message saying 'Read the text below:' will appear. When prompted, read the text aloud in a normal pace and tone of voice. You should now be able to hear your own voice clearly in your headphones. It is important that you speak continously once the reading phase starts, even if the voice feedback in your headphones makes this difficult.

Note: At this point, it is crucial that you have your headphones plugged in and equipped! Doing the test without headphones will cause an echo and may cause you to fail the test.

Important: This is a one-time test and cannot be retaken. Make sure you are completely ready before proceeding. Pressing the 'START TEST'-button will start the audio test immediately.
